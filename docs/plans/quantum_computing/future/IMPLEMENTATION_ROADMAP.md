## 🚧 **Risks & Mitigation**

### **Risk 1: Quantum Hardware Limitations**

**Risk**: Quantum hardware not ready for production use

**Mitigation**:
- Hybrid approach (classical fallback)
- Gradual integration
- Monitor hardware progress
- Adjust timeline as needed

### **Risk 2: High Costs**

**Risk**: Quantum computing costs too high

**Mitigation**:
- University partnerships for cost-effective access
- Selective use (only for complex calculations)
- Batch processing
- Cost optimization

### **Risk 3: Performance Issues**

**Risk**: Quantum computing slower than classical

**Mitigation**:
- Hybrid approach (quantum enhancement, not replacement)
- Async processing (non-blocking)
- Performance monitoring
- Optimization

### **Risk 4: Error Rates**

**Risk**: Quantum errors affect accuracy

**Mitigation**:
- Error correction codes
- Multiple shots (statistical averaging)
- Validation and testing
- Fallback to classical
