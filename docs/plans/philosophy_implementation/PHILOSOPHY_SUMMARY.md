## 🧠 **How the Key Works**

### **1. Always Learning With You**

The on-device world model learns **alongside** you:
- Which doors resonate with you (energy function, trained on real outcomes)
- When you're ready for new doors (MPC planner, context-aware timing)
- Where doors lead before you open them (transition predictor, learned from experience)
- How your taste evolves over time (taste drift detection, seasonal patterns)

**Not learning about you. Learning with you. And it starts from your very first interaction.**

**Chat is a turbo button, never a requirement.** A user who never chats with their AI approaches the same accuracy as a user who chats daily -- it just takes more observations. The model learns from how you live: visits, returns, dismissals, dwell time, temporal patterns.

---

### **2. Three Learning Modes**

#### **Personal Learning (On-Device)**
The key learns which doors YOU open:
- Which spots you visit, how long you stay, what you return to, what you dismiss
- Behavioral patterns: temporal rhythms, exploration vs. comfort cycles, life changes
- New users get pre-seeded intelligence from day one (trained on 100k+ real personality profiles)
- Even if you skip onboarding entirely, the model starts learning from your first tap

#### **AI2AI Learning (Peer-to-Peer)**
The key discovers people who open similar doors:
- Bluetooth connections with privacy-preserving vibe exchange
- Compatibility matching via energy function (learned, not formula-based)
- Agents can negotiate group activities when they find compatible people
- Federated learning: shared model improvements, never shared personal data

#### **Cloud Learning (Optional Enhancement)**
The key gains wisdom from the network:
- Which doors lead where (federated aggregate patterns)
- Popular vs. hidden doors (with differential privacy protection)
- Pre-trained model weights that make new users smart from day one
- All cloud sync uses post-quantum encryption (PQXDH/ML-KEM)

## 🎭 **Your Doors Stay Yours**

### **The Problem**
Without protection, everyone opens the same doors. You lose what makes you unique.

### **The Solution: Contextual Personality**

**Core Personality (Stable)**
- The doors that define YOU
- The energy function protects against homogenization by learning what makes YOU unique
- The transition predictor learns how much change is authentic growth vs. noise -- per person, not a fixed percentage
- Your authentic doors stay yours

**Contextual Layers (Flexible)**
- Work doors vs. social doors vs. exploration doors
- You open different doors in different contexts
- Adaptation without losing core identity

**Evolution Timeline (Preserved)**
- All doors you've ever opened
- Life phases preserved forever
- You can grow and change
- Your door history is never lost

**Result:** You can authentically change over time, but your doors stay yours. People can find you based on doors you're opening NOW or doors you opened THEN.

### **Human Condition Spectra (Undefined Traits)**
- AVRAI treats fuzzy human concepts as contextual spectrums, not fixed labels.
- Inferences are modeled as state/trait/phase with confidence, scope, and decay.
- Users can challenge beliefs with scoped overrides without destabilizing shared reality layers.
- Reference plan: `docs/plans/philosophy_implementation/HUMAN_CONDITION_SPECTRA_PLAN.md`

## 🎯 **Usage Pattern Tracking**

People use the key differently:
- Some want quick recommendations ("find me a restaurant")
- Some want community discovery (events, meetups, third places)
- Some want business partnerships and networking
- Most want different things at different times, depending on context

**The MPC planner adapts to YOUR usage pattern:**

**Recommendation Mode:** Quick, efficient when you need it  
**Community Mode:** Events, groups when you're receptive  
**Business Mode:** Partnerships, sponsorships, networking when you're in work mode  
**Exploration Mode:** When the model is uncertain about you, it prioritizes doors that teach it the most (active uncertainty reduction)

**Result:** The key shows you doors when you're ready, never overwhelming. And when it's unsure, it admits it and learns rather than guessing.

## 🏗️ **Eight Core Principles**

### **1. Offline-First**
The key works everywhere (subway, park, street), not just when online. The world model runs entirely on-device.

### **2. Always Learning With You**
The world model learns alongside you. Partnership, not prescription. Learns from behavior, never requires chat.

### **3. Privacy-Preserving**
Your door history is yours. Learning happens on-device. Post-quantum encryption (PQXDH/ML-KEM) protects communications against current AND future threats.

### **4. Authenticity Over Algorithms**
Your doors stay yours. The energy function learns what makes YOU unique. Growth without loss of identity.

### **5. User Empowerment**
You choose which doors to open. AVRAI provides access, you create outcomes.

### **6. Real World Enhancement**
Technology enhances real-world experiences, doesn't replace them. AI is used for the right reasons—better real-world experiences.

### **7. Chat-as-Accelerator, Never Gate**
A user who never chats approaches the same accuracy as a user who chats daily. Chat is a turbo button, not a requirement.

### **8. Intelligence Over Formulas**
Learned functions beat hardcoded formulas. Energy functions beat classifiers. Predictions beat static scores. The world model gets smarter with every interaction.
