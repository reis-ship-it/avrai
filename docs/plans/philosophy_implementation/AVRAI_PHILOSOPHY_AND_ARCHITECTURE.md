## 🧠 **How the AI Learns: Doors, Not Algorithms**

### **The Core Mechanism: Calling to Action**

**Users are called to action in the real world by being given spots, lists, places, events, etc. that would make their life better based on meaningful connections and positive influence.**

**The AI2AI system is the least invasive and most active and best way to make that happen.**

### **The Privacy & Legal Advantage**

**"At the end of the day, the AI2AI system is about user privacy and direct access to live data without encroaching legal issues."**

- **On-Device Processing:** All personal data stays on device. The world model trains ON your device, not in the cloud.
- **Anonymized Sharing:** Only anonymized vibe signatures and DP-protected gradients shared
- **Direct Access to Live Data:** Real-time insights without storing personal data
- **No Legal Encroachment:** GDPR compliance is a legal requirement (not a policy decision). Post-quantum encryption protects data today and against future quantum threats.
- **Post-Quantum Security:** Signal Protocol with PQXDH (ML-KEM/Kyber) ensures every conversation is protected even against future quantum computers. Your doors are locked with keys that can't be broken.

### **The World Model: How the AI Opens Doors**

The AI uses an on-device world model to understand your world and show you doors. The world model has four components:

1. **State Encoder** -- understands where you are NOW (personality, patterns, context, history, season, time of day)
2. **Energy Function (Critic)** -- evaluates "how good is this door for this person?" Lower energy = better door. Learned from real outcomes, not hardcoded formulas
3. **Transition Predictor** -- predicts "if you open this door, how will your world change?" Learned from how people actually evolve after opening doors
4. **MPC Planner** -- plans sequences of doors: "first this coffee shop, which leads to this community, which leads to this event." Not just one-shot recommendations, but door trajectories

**All four components run on-device, train from your own experiences, and work completely offline.**

### **Three Learning Modes**

#### **1. Personal Learning (On-Device, Offline)**
**What:** The world model learns which doors YOU open
- Which spots you visit, how long you stay, what you return to
- Which doors you dismiss (equally informative as doors you open)
- When you're receptive to new doors vs. when you want familiar ones
- How your taste evolves over seasons, life changes, and growth
- What brings you meaning — learned from behavior, not from what you say

**Philosophy:** Your personal AI is fully autonomous. It learns from your actions, not from trends or what others do. It discovers YOUR doors. You never have to chat with your AI for it to learn — it watches how you live and opens doors accordingly. Chat accelerates the learning, but is never required.

**Result:** Doors that match your authentic self, getting more accurate with every interaction

---

#### **2. AI2AI Learning (Peer-to-Peer, Offline)**
**What:** AI agents learn about people who open similar doors
- Discover others through Bluetooth
- Exchange personality insights (door preferences, anonymized)
- Calculate compatibility (door alignment via energy function)
- Learn from peer experiences (federated learning: shared model improvements, never shared personal data)
- Negotiate group activities when high compatibility is detected (agents plan joint door-opening)

**Philosophy:** AI2AI = Doors to People. When your AI connects with someone else's AI, it discovers that they open similar doors. Compatible AIs mean people who might open doors together. The agents can even plan group activities on behalf of their users — genuinely novel, no existing app does this.

**Result:** Community connections through shared door preferences, group activities planned by agents

---

#### **3. Cloud Learning (Network Intelligence, Optional)**
**What:** The world model gains collective wisdom about which doors lead where
- Aggregate patterns (popular doors, hidden gems) via privacy-preserving federated aggregation
- Collective insights (what's beyond each door)
- Pre-trained model weights (new users benefit from day one because the model ships with population-level intelligence from real personality data)
- Enhanced doors from network wisdom

**Philosophy:** Cloud is optional enhancement. Your personal AI works fully offline. Cloud adds network intelligence when available but isn't required. All cloud sync uses post-quantum encryption.

**Result:** Enhanced door discovery from collective wisdom, day-one intelligence for new users

## 🎭 **Personality: Your Doors Stay Yours**

### **The Problem: Homogenization**
Without protection, AI2AI learning makes everyone similar. Everyone opens the same doors. You lose what makes you unique.

### **The Solution: Contextual Personality System**

#### **1. Core Personality (Stable)**
**What:** The doors that define YOU
**Resistance:** The world model's transition predictor learns how much drift is authentic growth vs. surface noise for each individual. The energy function protects against homogenization by learning what makes YOU unique. (The original "30% max drift" was a starting heuristic; the world model learns the right threshold for each person.)
**Philosophy:** Your authentic doors stay yours

#### **2. Contextual Layers (Flexible)**
**What:** Different doors in different contexts
- Work doors (professional)
- Social doors (friends, exploration)
- Location doors (adapt to new places)
**Philosophy:** You open different doors in different contexts, but your core stays stable

#### **3. Evolution Timeline (Preserved)**
**What:** History of all doors you've opened
- Life phases preserved forever
- Authentic transformations recorded
- Never deleted
**Philosophy:** You can grow and change, but your door history is never lost

### **Result: Authentic Growth Without Homogenization**
- Your doors stay yours
- You can authentically change over time
- People can find you based on doors you open NOW or doors you opened THEN
- Everyone maintains uniqueness

## 📊 **The 12 Dimensions: Understanding Your Doors**

### **Why 12 Dimensions?**
The AI needs to understand which doors YOU are ready to open. Not everyone opens the same doors.

### **The Dimensions (8 Original + 4 New)**

#### **Original 8: Discovery Style**
1. `exploration_eagerness` - Willingness to try new door types
2. `community_orientation` - Solo doors vs. group doors
3. `authenticity_preference` - Hidden gem doors vs. popular doors
4. `social_discovery_style` - How you find doors through others
5. `temporal_flexibility` - Planned doors vs. spontaneous doors
6. `location_adventurousness` - How far you'll go for doors
7. `curation_tendency` - How much you share doors with others
8. `trust_network_reliance` - How much you trust others' door recommendations

#### **New 4: Experience Preferences**
9. `energy_preference` - Chill doors vs. high-energy doors
10. `novelty_seeking` - New doors always vs. favorite doors repeatedly
11. `value_orientation` - Budget doors vs. premium doors
12. `crowd_tolerance` - Quiet/intimate doors vs. bustling/popular doors

### **Result: Precision Door Matching**
The AI understands YOUR door preferences across 12 dimensions, enabling precise matching for:
- Spot recommendations (your individual doors)
- Community discovery (doors your community opens)
- Event suggestions (new doors at familiar places)
- People matching (people who open similar doors)
- List compatibility (curated collections of doors that match your taste)
- Business partnerships (doors between businesses and experts)
- Sponsorships (doors between brands and events)

**Note:** The 12 dimensions are the human-interpretable personality model used for UI/UX (explaining recommendations, personality insights). The world model's state encoder uses ~145-155 total features including these 12 plus quantum vibe states, knot invariants, location context, temporal patterns, behavioral trajectory, and more. A future JEPA (Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture) may discover a learned embedding that's even better at predicting which doors resonate -- but the 12 interpretable dimensions will always remain for user-facing features.
