## Cost Optimization Strategies

### Immediate Cost Savings

1. **Use Existing Hardware:** If you already have a Mac, save $2,000 - $3,500
2. **File Provisional Patents First:** Save $22,200 - $82,400 vs. non-provisional
3. **Use Free Service Tiers:** Most services offer generous free tiers for development
4. **Defer Security Audit:** Can wait until post-seed or first revenue
5. **DIY Legal Documents:** Use templates for Privacy Policy/ToS initially (upgrade later)

### Long-Term Cost Management

1. **Monitor API Usage:** Set up billing alerts to avoid surprise charges
2. **Optimize Cloud Costs:** Use caching, CDN, and efficient queries
3. **Batch Patent Filings:** Some attorneys offer discounts for multiple patents
4. **Negotiate Service Contracts:** Annual plans often cheaper than monthly
5. **Consider Small Entity Status:** 50% discount on USPTO fees if eligible
