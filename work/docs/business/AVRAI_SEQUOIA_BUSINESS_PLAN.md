## 6. Business Model & Monetization Strategy
AVRAI abandons standard subscription and ad-banner models. The value is in the network, the data scaffolding, and the mesh. 

### Revenue Stream 1: Zero-Knowledge Spatial Advertising & Intelligence
Because AVRAI uses Epidemic Routing to pass "Pheromones" (Knowledge Vectors) from phone to phone physically, we enable B2B monetization models that are impossible for traditional cloud-based platforms.

* **The Pheromone Ad Network:** A local business (e.g., an indie bookstore) pays to drop a 2KB "Digital Pheromone." As users walk by, their local DNA string mathematically accepts or rejects the ad. If accepted, the user carries the ad across town, spreading it to other highly compatible users. 
  * **Pheromone Decay & Premium Persistence:** By default, Pheromones have a short "Time-To-Live" (TTL) and fade quickly to keep the mesh network clean. However, businesses can pay a premium to increase the half-life of their Pheromone, allowing it to persist and hop across the city for days or weeks. 
  * **Anti-Hacking Cryptography:** Premium, long-lasting Pheromones will become a massive target for bad actors trying to artificially sustain or spoof free ads. Therefore, every premium Pheromone is cryptographically signed by the Locality Agent. If a bad actor modifies the TTL or tries to clone it, the signature breaks, and the Swarm's `GovernanceKernelService` instantly drops the packet.
  * **Model:** Businesses pay a micro-transaction ($0.05) every time their Pheromone successfully hops between two compatible devices, plus a premium for extended TTL. They receive zero personal data—only a geographic heatmap showing how far their "vibe" spread.
* **Proof-of-Presence Attribution:** Current digital ads cannot prove a user actually walked into a store. AVRAI's deterministic math knows if an ad pheromone actually resulted in a physical dwell event. **Model:** Businesses pay a premium for true ROI analytics—100% physical conversion attribution with 0% PII exposure.
* **Smart City / Event Heatmaps:** Large event organizers (e.g., SXSW, Coachella) or smart cities pay to understand how the "swarm" naturally flows through physical spaces. **Model:** B2B subscriptions to view aggregate knot trajectories to optimize infrastructure, traffic flow, and vendor placement.

### Revenue Stream 2: The AI Sandbox "Sockets" (DaaS for Labs)
AVRAI sells API access to foundational AI research labs (e.g., Meta FAIR, university labs). Researchers pay to plug their experimental AI architectures into AVRAI's real-world environment.
* **The Memory Socket:** Labs buy anonymized `(State) -> (Action) -> (Next State)` episodic memory sequences to train Liquid Neural Networks and Energy-Based Models.
* **The Mathematical Socket:** Quantum researchers buy access to our continuous float vectors to map Hilbert space interference.
* **Model:** Enterprise licensing tiers ($250k - $2M+/year) for direct pipeline access to the "Reality Engine" data, completely abstracted via the "Air Gap" to ensure zero liability regarding PII.
