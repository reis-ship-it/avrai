## Getting Started

### What is the Reservation System?

The Reservation System in SPOTS allows you to:

- **Create reservations** at restaurants, venues, events, and businesses
- **Manage your reservations** in one place
- **View analytics** about your reservation patterns and compatibility
- **Join waitlists** when capacity is full
- **Get automatic reminders** for your reservations

### Key Features

✅ **Offline-First**: Works completely offline - all your reservations are stored locally  
✅ **Privacy by Design**: Your data is protected with advanced privacy features  
✅ **Smart Matching**: AI-powered compatibility matching helps you find the perfect spots  
✅ **Automatic Reminders**: Get reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before your reservation  
✅ **Flexible Management**: Modify or cancel reservations with clear policies  

### First Time Using Reservations?

1. **Sign in** to your SPOTS account
2. **Navigate** to the Reservations section (from the home page or menu)
3. **Create your first reservation** at a spot, business, or event
4. **View your reservations** in "My Reservations"

## Creating a Reservation

### Step 1: Choose What You Want to Reserve

You can create reservations for:

- **Spots**: Restaurants, bars, venues, cafes
- **Businesses**: Businesses with reservation systems
- **Events**: Workshops, classes, concerts, meetups

### Step 2: Select Date & Time

1. **Tap** on the date picker to choose your date
2. **Select** a time from available time slots
3. The system automatically checks **availability** and shows available times

**Tip:** Time slots are filtered based on:
- Business hours
- Existing reservations
- Capacity limits

### Step 3: Set Party Size

1. **Enter** the number of people in your party
2. For events, you may need to specify **ticket count** separately
3. The system checks **capacity** automatically

**Group Reservations:**
- For parties of 2 or more, SPOTS automatically creates a "fabric" to track group compatibility
- This helps match you with compatible group experiences

### Step 4: Add Special Requests (Optional)

You can add special requests such as:

- Dietary restrictions ("Vegetarian options please")
- Seating preferences ("Window seat preferred")
- Accessibility needs ("Wheelchair accessible")
- Other notes for the business

**Privacy Note:** Special requests are shared with the business only if you explicitly consent.

### Step 5: Review & Confirm

1. **Review** your reservation details:
   - Location/Event name
   - Date and time
   - Party size
   - Special requests
   - **Compatibility score** (if available)
   - **Pricing** (if applicable)

2. **Tap "Create Reservation"**

3. **Payment** (if required):
   - If the reservation requires payment, you'll be prompted to pay
   - Payment is processed securely
   - Free reservations are automatically confirmed

### Step 6: Confirmation

Once your reservation is created:

✅ **Confirmation notification** is sent immediately  
✅ **Reminders** are automatically scheduled (24h and 1h before)  
✅ **Reservation details** are saved offline  
✅ **Reservation appears** in "My Reservations"

## Managing Reservations

### Modifying Reservations

You can modify reservations to change:

- **Date and time**
- **Party size**
- **Ticket count** (for events)
- **Special requests**

#### How to Modify

1. **Open** the reservation details page
2. **Tap "Modify"**
3. **Make your changes**
4. **Review** and confirm

#### Modification Limits

**Important:** Reservations have modification limits:

- ✅ **Maximum 3 modifications** per reservation
- ✅ **Must be at least 24 hours** before reservation time
- ❌ **Cannot modify** within 24 hours of reservation time

**Why limits?** To prevent abuse and respect business capacity planning.

**Modification Count Display:**
- You'll see how many modifications you've made (e.g., "2 of 3 modifications used")
- The system prevents modification if you've reached the limit

#### What Happens When You Modify

1. **Availability check**: System checks if new time/party size is available
2. **Capacity update**: Capacity is adjusted for the new party size
3. **Notification**: Business is notified of the change
4. **Confirmation**: You receive confirmation of the modification

---

### Cancelling Reservations

You can cancel reservations at any time, but **cancellation policies** apply.

#### How to Cancel

1. **Open** the reservation details page
2. **Tap "Cancel Reservation"**
3. **Enter cancellation reason** (optional)
4. **Review cancellation policy**:
   - Refund eligibility
   - Cancellation fees (if any)
   - Refund amount (if applicable)
5. **Confirm cancellation**

#### Cancellation Policy

Each reservation has a **cancellation policy** that determines:

- **Refund eligibility**: Full refund, partial refund, or no refund
- **Cancellation window**: How far in advance you must cancel
- **Cancellation fees**: Any fees that apply

**Default Policy:**
- ✅ **24-hour cancellation window**: Cancel at least 24 hours before reservation
- ✅ **Full refund**: If cancelled within the window
- ❌ **No refund**: If cancelled within 24 hours (unless extenuating circumstances)

**Business-Specific Policies:**
- Some businesses may have different policies (e.g., 48-hour window, partial refunds)
- Always check the policy before cancelling

#### Refund Processing

If you're eligible for a refund:

1. **Refund is processed** automatically after cancellation
2. **Processing time**: 3-5 business days (varies by payment method)
3. **Notification**: You'll receive confirmation when refund is processed
4. **Check refund status**: View refund status in reservation details

#### Cancellation Reasons

You can specify a cancellation reason:

- Change of plans
- Illness
- Emergency
- Other (with details)

**Extenuating Circumstances:**
- For extenuating circumstances (injury, death in family, etc.), you may be eligible for a full refund even if outside the cancellation window
- Contact support for assistance with extenuating circumstances

### Calendar Integration

Sync your reservations to your device calendar (iOS Calendar, Google Calendar) to keep track of all your bookings in one place.

#### Syncing to Calendar

1. **Open** a reservation detail page

**Calendar Event Includes:**

#### Removing from Calendar

To remove a reservation from your calendar:

### Recurring Reservations

Create reservations that repeat automatically on a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom interval).

#### Creating a Recurring Reservation

1. **Create** a reservation as normal
2. **Before confirming**, tap **"Make Recurring"**
3. **Select pattern**:
   - **Daily**: Every day, every 2 days, etc.
   - **Weekly**: Every week on specific days (e.g., every Friday)
   - **Monthly**: Every month on the same date
   - **Custom**: Custom interval (e.g., every 3 weeks)
4. **Set end condition**:
   - **End date**: Recurring until a specific date
   - **Max occurrences**: Recurring for a specific number of times
5. **Review** preview of generated reservations
6. **Confirm**: All recurring instances are created

**Example:** Create a weekly reservation every Friday at 7 PM for 10 weeks.

#### Managing Recurring Series

**View Recurring Series:**
- Navigate to "Recurring Reservations" page
- See all your recurring series with pattern and instance count

**Pause Series:**
- Pause a recurring series to temporarily stop creating new instances
- Existing reservations remain active
- Resume later to continue the series

**Cancel Series:**
- Cancel a recurring series to stop all future instances
- Option to cancel existing reservations or keep them
- Cancellation policies apply to each instance

**Modify Series:**
- Modify the base reservation template
- Changes apply to future instances only
- Existing reservations remain unchanged

#### Recurring Reservation Benefits

✅ **Time Saving**: Create multiple reservations at once  
✅ **Consistency**: Never miss your regular reservations  
✅ **Flexibility**: Pause, resume, or cancel anytime  
✅ **Smart Scheduling**: System suggests optimal times based on your patterns

### Sharing & Transferring Reservations

Share reservations with friends or family, or transfer ownership to another user.

#### Sharing a Reservation

**Share with Read-Only Access:**
- Recipient can view reservation details
- Cannot modify or cancel
- Useful for showing someone your reservation

**Share with Full Access:**
- Recipient can view, modify, and cancel
- Useful for group coordination
- Original owner retains ownership

**How to Share:**
1. **Open** reservation detail page
2. **Tap "Share Reservation"**
3. **Search** for user to share with
4. **Select permission level** (read-only or full access)
5. **Confirm**: Reservation is shared

**Shared Reservation Features:**
- Both owner and shared user see the reservation
- Shared user receives notifications
- Compatibility prediction shown when sharing

#### Transferring a Reservation

Transfer ownership of a reservation to another user (irreversible).

**When to Transfer:**
- You can't attend and want to give it to someone else
- Gift a reservation to a friend
- Change of plans

**How to Transfer:**
1. **Open** reservation detail page
2. **Tap "Transfer Reservation"**
3. **Search** for user to transfer to
4. **Review** compatibility prediction
5. **Confirm**: Ownership is transferred

**Important Warnings:**
- ⚠️ **Irreversible**: Transfer cannot be undone
- ⚠️ **You lose access**: Original owner loses all access after transfer
- ⚠️ **Payment**: If paid reservation, payment is not refunded (new owner receives reservation)

**Compatibility Prediction:**
- System shows predicted compatibility (0-100%) when transferring
- Based on personality profiles and reservation history
- Higher compatibility = better match for the reservation

#### Revoking Sharing

To revoke sharing access:
1. **Open** reservation detail page
2. **Tap "Manage Sharing"**
3. **Tap "Revoke"** next to shared user
4. **Confirm**: Sharing access is revoked

**Note:** Transferring ownership cannot be revoked - it's permanent.

## Waitlists

### What is a Waitlist?

When a spot, business, or event is **fully booked**, you can join the **waitlist**. If capacity becomes available, you'll be automatically promoted to a confirmed reservation.

### How to Join a Waitlist

1. **Try to create a reservation** when capacity is full
2. **System shows**: "Fully booked - Join waitlist?"
3. **Enter your details**: Date, time, party size
4. **Tap "Join Waitlist"**
5. **Confirm**: You're added to the waitlist with a **position number**

### Your Waitlist Position

- **Position number**: Shows where you are in line (e.g., "Position 3")
- **Position updates**: Your position may change as others are promoted or leave the waitlist
- **Check position**: View your position in reservation details

### When Capacity Becomes Available

If capacity becomes available:

1. **Automatic promotion**: You're automatically promoted to a confirmed reservation
2. **Notification**: You receive a notification that you've been promoted
3. **Confirmation**: Your reservation is confirmed (payment may be required)
4. **Reminders**: Automatic reminders are scheduled

**Important:** Promotion is **first-come-first-served** based on when you joined the waitlist.

### Leaving the Waitlist

You can leave the waitlist at any time:

1. **Open** the waitlist entry details
2. **Tap "Leave Waitlist"**
3. **Confirm**: You're removed from the waitlist

**Note:** Leaving the waitlist is permanent - you can't rejoin at the same position.

## Payment & Refunds

### Payment Processing

#### Paid Reservations

Some reservations require payment:

- **Event tickets**: Often require upfront payment
- **Restaurant deposits**: Some restaurants require deposits
- **Special events**: Premium events may require payment

#### Payment Methods

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Processed securely via Stripe
- **Payment is processed** immediately when you create the reservation
- **Payment confirmation** is sent via email and in-app

#### Pricing Display

Before creating a reservation, you'll see:

- **Ticket price**: Price per ticket (if applicable)
- **Total price**: Total amount (ticket price × ticket count)
- **Platform fee**: SPOTS platform fee (10% of ticket fee, if applicable)
- **Deposit amount**: Deposit amount (if applicable)

### Refunds

#### When You're Eligible for a Refund

- ✅ **Cancelled within cancellation window**: Full or partial refund per policy
- ✅ **Business cancellation**: Full refund if business cancels
- ✅ **Event cancellation**: Full refund if event is cancelled
- ❌ **No-show**: No refund if you don't show up

#### Refund Processing

1. **Cancellation**: When you cancel an eligible reservation
2. **Automatic processing**: Refund is processed automatically
3. **Processing time**: 3-5 business days (varies by payment method)
4. **Notification**: You receive confirmation when refund is processed

#### Refund Amount

Refund amount depends on:

- **Cancellation policy**: Full refund, partial refund, or no refund
- **Cancellation fees**: Any fees that apply
- **Time of cancellation**: Whether within the cancellation window

**Example:**
- **Full refund policy**: Cancel 24+ hours before → 100% refund
- **Partial refund policy**: Cancel 24+ hours before → 50% refund
- **No refund policy**: Cancel within 24 hours → 0% refund (unless extenuating circumstances)

#### Checking Refund Status

1. **Open** the cancelled reservation details
2. **View refund status**: "Processing", "Completed", or "Failed"
3. **See refund amount**: Amount refunded
4. **Contact support**: If refund is delayed or failed

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Reservation Not Found"

**Problem:** Can't find a reservation you created.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check filters**: Make sure filters aren't hiding your reservation
2. **Refresh**: Pull down to refresh the reservations list
3. **Check offline**: Reservations are stored offline - sync when online
4. **Contact support**: If still not found, contact support

#### "Cannot Modify Reservation"

**Problem:** Modification button is disabled or shows an error.

**Reasons:**
- ✅ **Modification limit reached**: You've used all 3 modifications
- ✅ **Too close to time**: Within 24 hours of reservation time
- ✅ **Reservation cancelled**: Can't modify cancelled reservations

**Solutions:**
- **Check modification count**: View how many modifications you've made
- **Check reservation time**: Must be at least 24 hours before
- **Cancel and rebook**: If needed, cancel and create a new reservation

#### "Payment Failed"

**Problem:** Payment didn't go through when creating a reservation.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check payment method**: Make sure your card is valid and has funds
2. **Retry**: Try creating the reservation again
3. **Update payment method**: Update your payment method in settings
4. **Contact support**: If payment continues to fail

#### "Waitlist Position Not Updating"

**Problem:** Waitlist position seems stuck.

**Reasons:**
- **Normal behavior**: Position only changes when others are promoted or leave
- **Cache issue**: Position may need to refresh

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh**: Pull down to refresh the waitlist position
2. **Wait**: Position updates are automatic - wait for notifications
3. **Check notifications**: You'll be notified if you're promoted

#### "Reminders Not Received"

**Problem:** Not getting reminder notifications.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check notification settings**: Make sure notifications are enabled for SPOTS
2. **Check device settings**: Make sure device notifications are enabled
3. **Check reservation time**: Reminders are sent 24h and 1h before
4. **Verify reservation**: Make sure reservation is confirmed

#### "Offline Mode Issues"

**Problem:** Issues when offline.

**Solutions:**
1. **Offline-first**: Reservations work offline - data is stored locally
2. **Sync when online**: Reservations sync automatically when you go online
3. **Check connectivity**: Make sure you're connected when syncing
4. **Force sync**: Pull down to force sync when online

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

#### What types of reservations can I create?

You can create reservations for:
- **Spots**: Restaurants, bars, venues, cafes
- **Businesses**: Businesses with reservation systems
- **Events**: Workshops, classes, concerts, meetups

#### How do I know if a reservation is confirmed?

Check the **status badge** on your reservation:
- **Pending**: Awaiting confirmation
- **Confirmed**: ✅ Confirmed and ready
- **Cancelled**: Cancelled
- **Completed**: Reservation fulfilled

#### Can I create multiple reservations for the same time?

Yes, you can create multiple reservations, but:
- The system checks for **existing reservations** at the same time/location
- You'll see a warning if you already have a reservation for that time
- This helps prevent accidental double bookings

#### What happens if I don't show up?

If you don't show up (no-show):
- ❌ **No refund** (unless extenuating circumstances)
- ⚠️ **May affect** your ability to reserve at that location in the future
- 📧 **Notification** sent to the business

### Modification & Cancellation

#### How many times can I modify a reservation?

- **Maximum 3 modifications** per reservation
- **Must be at least 24 hours** before reservation time
- After 3 modifications, you'll need to cancel and create a new reservation

#### Can I cancel a free reservation?

Yes, you can cancel free reservations at any time. Cancellation policies still apply (business-specific policies may vary).

#### What's the difference between cancellation and dispute?

- **Cancellation**: You choose to cancel (applies cancellation policy)
- **Dispute**: For extenuating circumstances (injury, death, etc.) - may get full refund even if outside cancellation window

### Waitlists

#### How does the waitlist work?

1. **Join waitlist** when capacity is full
2. **Get position** in line
3. **Automatic promotion** when capacity becomes available (first-come-first-served)
4. **Notification** when promoted

#### Can I join multiple waitlists?

Yes, you can join multiple waitlists for different dates/times.

#### What happens if I'm promoted from the waitlist?

- **Automatic reservation** is created
- **Payment** may be required (if applicable)
- **Notification** sent
- **Reminders** scheduled automatically

### Payments & Refunds

#### How are payments processed?

Payments are processed securely via **Stripe**. Your payment information is never stored by SPOTS.

#### When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed **automatically** after cancellation:
- **Processing time**: 3-5 business days
- **Method**: Refunded to original payment method
- **Notification**: You'll receive confirmation

#### Are there any fees?

- **Platform fee**: 10% of ticket fee (if applicable)
- **Cancellation fees**: Per cancellation policy (business-specific)

### Privacy & Data

#### How is my data protected?

- **Privacy by Design**: Uses advanced privacy features (agentId system)
- **Offline-first**: Data is stored locally on your device
- **Optional sharing**: User data is only shared with businesses if you consent

#### Can businesses see my personal information?

Only if you **explicitly consent**:
- **By default**: Businesses see minimal information (party size, reservation time)
- **With consent**: You can share name, email, phone (for contact purposes)
- **Privacy**: Your actual user ID is never shared (uses agentId internally)

### Analytics & Compatibility

#### What is compatibility score?

Compatibility score is an AI-powered metric that shows how well a location/event matches your preferences, based on:
- Your personality profile
- Past reservation patterns
- Location characteristics
- Quantum matching algorithms

#### How is compatibility calculated?

Compatibility uses:
- **Quantum matching**: Deep compatibility analysis
- **Knot theory**: Group compatibility (for group reservations)
- **AI2AI learning**: Learning from past experiences

#### Can I improve my compatibility?

Yes! Compatibility improves as you:
- Make more reservations
- Complete reservations successfully
- Provide feedback
- Use the system more

### Technical Questions

#### Does the app work offline?

Yes! **Offline-first design**:
- All reservations are stored locally
- Create, view, and manage reservations offline
- Automatic sync when online

#### How do I sync my reservations?

Sync happens **automatically** when you're online. You can also:
- **Pull down** to force refresh
- **Go online** - sync happens automatically

#### What devices are supported?

- **iOS**: iPhone, iPad
- **Android**: Phones and tablets
- **Web**: Browser-based access (coming soon)

### Support

#### How do I contact support?

- **In-app**: Settings → Support → Contact Us
- **Support**: https://avrai.org/support

#### What information should I include when contacting support?

- **Reservation ID**: Found in reservation details
- **Issue description**: What problem you're experiencing
- **Screenshots**: If applicable
- **Account email**: Your SPOTS account email
