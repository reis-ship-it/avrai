## Why This Batch Matters to AVRAI

This batch reinforces a single direction: AVRAI should learn through closed-loop evidence, maintain explicit conviction accounting, and safely integrate internal + external + third-party data without allowing unverified claims to bypass world-model validation.

The value is not a new one-off subsystem; it is stronger cross-phase wiring of data quality, feedback loops, model update discipline, and planner behavior.

## Guardrail Position

- External research can influence priors and candidate generation.
- External research cannot directly force production behavior.
- Promotion remains bounded by internal outcome validation, contradiction checks, and rollback readiness.

This maintains AVRAI's desired reality model: self-learning, self-questioning, self-healing, and self-improvement with evidence-bound conviction.
