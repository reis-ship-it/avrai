Date: 2026-04-01

Master Plan refs: 10.9.12, 10.9.22, 10.9.23, 10.9.24, 10.9.25

## Scope

Follow `M31-P10-8` with a runtime/admin/reality-model slice that treats each city pack as a versioned living simulation substrate instead of a one-off seed bundle. Simulations for any operator-selected supported place should hydrate from the latest available AVRAI evidence across app, runtime/OS, and governed reality-model outputs while preserving lineage, freshness, and rollback-safe history.

## Explicit Objectives

1. treat `cityPackStructuralRef` as a stable versioned identity while allowing sidecars, realism layers, and latest-state simulation inputs to refresh continuously over time
2. hydrate simulations from the newest available AVRAI evidence for the chosen supported place across app observations, runtime/OS intake and locality state, and governed reality-model learning outputs
3. make city-pack refresh explicit and auditable: every refreshed simulation basis must preserve evidence lineage, freshness receipts, and the prior pack/version it supersedes or extends
4. allow `World Simulation Lab` to boot against the latest available state for a chosen place instead of only earlier static seed packs, while still supporting replayable historical versions
5. keep denied or low-confidence evidence visible to the daemon as labeled learning input without allowing it to silently rewrite the current served city-pack basis
6. preserve time-validity windows and atomic-time coherence metadata where available, without making true quantum-hardware execution a prerequisite for the loop

## Guardrails

1. this follow-on starts only after `M31-P10-8` closeout
2. city packs must be versioned and rollback-safe; they may not silently mutate in place without recorded lineage
3. the latest-state simulation basis must be freshness-bounded and evidence-linked; stale or weakly sourced inputs must fail closed or degrade explicitly
4. only governed reality-model outputs may enter the reality-model contribution lane; raw simulation artifacts and denied lab outcomes remain labeled evidence, not silent truth promotion
5. operator-selected places are limited to places supported by available AVRAI data; the loop must not fabricate unsupported place coverage
6. atomic-time or quantum-time validity metadata is additive coherence/receipt context, not a license to bypass current classical/native execution and governance controls
