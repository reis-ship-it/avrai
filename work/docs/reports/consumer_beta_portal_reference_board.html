Portal Principles

The beta atlas currently documents neutral white mobile frames. This review page isolates the visual moves that should be present once those same flows inherit the full portal system.

World Every screen sits over a living environment.

Sky, cloud, grass, and night states are not decorative wallpaper. They set depth and orientation.

Container The slate is the product surface.

Blurred glass, metallic border, soft interior shadow, and negative border around the device frame.

Typography Headlines feel machined, not flat.

Satin Titanium heading treatment carries the sense of craft and precision across hero moments.

Maps Maps become recessed instruments.

Deep wells, vignettes, crosshair logic, and glowing pins replace light neutral cards with embedded maps.

Beta Atlas To Portal Pass

Background And Depth

Move from flat light grey phone canvases to a world-backed composition with visible breathing room.

Neutral white screens on soft grey surface with no atmospheric depth.

World visible around the slate, day or night sky mood, and depth established before content.

Surfaces And Borders

Replace ordinary cards with translucent glass containers and stronger edge logic.

Rounded white cards with low-contrast borders and flat hierarchy.

Blurred glass, interior shading, metallic border cues, and fewer but more intentional layers.

Typography And Brand Moments

Promote the branded surface language in entry points and key destinations.

Standard dark text with limited distinction between product chrome and body copy.

Metallic avrai headings, sharper title hierarchy, and calmer low-contrast secondary copy.

Maps And Data Wells

High-value spatial modules should feel embedded and instrument-like.

Maps behave like a normal card or placeholder block within the flow.

Recessed map well, vignettes, crosshair logic, and green pins against dark terrain styling.

Navigation Chrome

Chrome should feel engineered, not generic mobile scaffolding.

Conventional nav bars, pills, and utility icons on light backgrounds.

Lightweight metallic or translucent chrome floating above the slate with deliberate accent use.

Motion And State

Portal screens gain their feel from world motion, shimmer, and focus behavior.

Static white mock frames imply little about animation or environmental response.

Subtle world movement, shimmer in metallic type, turbine loading, and deeper focus mode states.

Review Prompts By Lane

Auth
Make entry feel like opening the world.

Login and signup should shift from plain form stacks toward a slate-on-world composition with branded chrome.

Onboarding
Keep the funnel calm, but atmospheric.

Questionnaire and consent can stay structured while inheriting portal depth, metallic headers, and stronger progress affordances.

Discovery
The home shell should justify the portal system.

Home, explore, map, and AI surfaces need the full recessed-map and floating-slate treatment more than any other lane.

Objects
Detail pages should feel instrumented.

Spot, list, event, and reservation flows should read as machined modules with embedded media and stronger chrome hierarchy.
